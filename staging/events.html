Convention Center

Perched in the heart of Bayangan Hotel lies one of Zamboanga’s most iconic event venues the Bayangan Hotel Convention Centre.
Representing a classic culmination of comfort-oriented and stylish interiors, this spacious multi-purpose venue comprises bright and versatile spaces designed to hold several events simultaneously. With a capacity to accommodate 300 people, the Bayangan Hotel Convention Centre prides itself on its cutting-edge visual and lighting options, regal seating set-ups, and fantastic catering options. Ideal for large corporate events, banquets, gala dinners, business meetings, and more, this multipurpose space is as dynamic as they come.
