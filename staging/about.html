About us

Welcome to Bayangan Hotel & Beach Resort, a breathtaking haven nestled in the heart of Labason, Zamboanga del Norte. Owned and operated by DIASTARS PROPERTY VENTURES, INC., we take pride in providing unparalleled hospitality and unforgettable experiences to our cherished guests.
Located in the serene Brgy. Osukan, our resort boasts an enviable beachfront location, offering panoramic views of the sparkling azure waters of the sea. The pristine white sands beckon you to relax, unwind, and let your worries melt away as you bask in the warm tropical sun. Whether you're seeking a romantic getaway, a family vacation, or a solo retreat, Bayangan Hotel & Beach Resort has something to offer everyone. Our accommodations are designed with your comfort and indulgence in mind. Each room and suite is elegantly appointed, featuring modern amenities and thoughtful touches that create a sense of luxury and serenity. Wake up to breathtaking sunrises or fall asleep to the soothing sound of waves crashing against the shore – the choice is yours.
